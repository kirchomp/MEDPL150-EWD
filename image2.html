This piece is similar to my first photo in the long-exposure style, but I included ONLY my hand, moving over the top of a bench. The effect was cool, but again, the original photograph was a bit overexposed, washed out. So in Photoshop, I added a Levels and a Curves adjustment layer so that my hand was more visible in the frame. It had an effect I didn't predict though, with the texture of the bench becoming more graphic and contrasted in the edited version compared to the original.
